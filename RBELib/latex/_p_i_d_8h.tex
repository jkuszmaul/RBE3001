Calculate the Feed-\/\-Forward term. 

Calculates the output necessary to keep the specified link in some pre-\/determined \char`\"{}neutral\char`\"{} state. At its simplist, this will perform a rudimentary gravity compensation. A more sophisticated feed-\/forward function would also handle motion profiling or the such.
